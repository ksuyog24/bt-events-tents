A Legacy of Celebration in Dodhara Chandani

Established in 2070 B.S., BT Events and Tents started with a simple mission: to bring high-quality event infrastructure to our local community.

We have grown from a small tent supplier to a full-service event partner. Whether it's the thumping bass of our sound systems or the elegance of our marquees, we ensure your event—be it a marriage, festival, or corporate meet—is unforgettable.
